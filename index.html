About

One good thing to come out of the COVID lockdowns in 2020 was my new-found love for hiking. With limited entertainment, my family and I started exploring new places in the countryside surrounding London. What started as a day out to Box Hill in Surrey quickly spiralled into a new hobby, my adventures of which are documented here.

There's also a story behind the blog's name. While coding this site, I was listening to Joanna Newsom, an artist I discovered a few months back. 'Leaving the City' is the name of one of her songs, and I thought it fit here too!
